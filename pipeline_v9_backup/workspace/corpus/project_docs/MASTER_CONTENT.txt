ADDENDUM NO. 1
MERIDIAN-1 DATA CENTRE, HALL A — ADDENDUM NO. 1 (ADD-001)

Date: 2026-03-02 · Issued by the Engineer. This Addendum forms part of the Contract Documents and modifies the Issued-for-Construction specifications as noted. Submittals reviewed against superseded requirements shall be revised and resubmitted.

Item 1 — Section 23 64 26, Clause 2.1 PERFORMANCE

Delete "not less than 6.0" and substitute "not less than 6.3". Revised clause reads: Coefficient of performance (COP) at AHRI 550/590 standard rating conditions shall be not less than 6.3 at 100 percent load.

Item 2 — Section 26 33 53, Clause 2.7 EFFICIENCY VERIFICATION

The referenced test method is amended to IEC 62040-3:2021, with efficiency verified at unity output power factor.

SECTION 21 22 00 - CLEAN-AGENT FIRE SUPPRESSION
MERIDIAN-1 DATA CENTRE, HALL A

Project No. MER-1-2026 · Issued for Construction · Revision 0 · 2026-01-15

SECTION 21 22 00 — CLEAN-AGENT FIRE SUPPRESSION

21 22 00 Part 1 — GENERAL

1.1 SUMMARY. Total-flooding clean-agent suppression for the Hall A data hall, UPS rooms, and battery rooms.

1.2 REFERENCES. NFPA 2001, UL 2166, IS 15493.

1.3 SUBMITTALS. Hydraulic calculations, nozzle listing data, agent quantity calculations, room integrity test procedure.

21 22 00 Part 2 — PRODUCTS

2.1 AGENT. FK-5-1-12 (fluoroketone). Recycled or reclaimed agent is not permitted.

2.2 DISCHARGE TIME. Agent discharge to 95 percent of design concentration shall complete within 10 seconds per NFPA 2001. [3]

2.3 DESIGN CONCENTRATION. Minimum design concentration 5.6 percent for Class C hazards at the protected enclosure's minimum ambient temperature.

2.4 ENCLOSURE INTEGRITY. Peak positive pressure during discharge shall not exceed 25 Pa with venting as designed. A door-fan integrity test per NFPA 2001 Annex C is required for each protected enclosure prior to acceptance; retention time not less than 10 minutes.

2.5 STORAGE. Cylinder charging pressure 24.8 bar (360 psi) at 21 degrees C, super-pressurised with nitrogen.

Footnote [3]: For protected volumes exceeding 2,000 cubic metres, a discharge time not exceeding 12 seconds is acceptable where extended-discharge nozzles covered by the manufacturer's UL 2166 listing are provided and hydraulic calculations demonstrate 95 percent concentration within 12 seconds.

21 22 00 Part 3 — EXECUTION

3.1 TESTS. Room integrity door-fan test results and puff test records are quality records and prerequisites to Level 4 testing.

SECTION 23 64 26 - WATER-COOLED CENTRIFUGAL CHILLERS
MERIDIAN-1 DATA CENTRE, HALL A

Project No. MER-1-2026 · Issued for Construction · Revision 0 · 2026-01-15

SECTION 23 64 26 — WATER-COOLED CENTRIFUGAL CHILLERS

23 64 26 Part 1 — GENERAL

1.1 SUMMARY. Water-cooled centrifugal chillers CH-A1 and CH-A2 serving the Hall A chilled water plant, N+1.

1.2 REFERENCES. AHRI 550/590, ASME B31.9, ASHRAE 15, ISO 10816-3.

1.3 SUBMITTALS. Selection data at AHRI conditions, sound data per AHRI 575, refrigerant declaration, and weld inspection plan for chilled water piping connections.

23 64 26 Part 2 — PRODUCTS

2.1 PERFORMANCE. Coefficient of performance (COP) at AHRI 550/590 standard rating conditions shall be not less than 6.0 at 100 percent load.

2.2 CAPACITY. Each chiller shall deliver not less than 1200 TR at design conditions with zero fouling allowance deducted.

2.3 SOUND. Sound pressure level shall not exceed 85 dBA at 1 metre per AHRI 575.

2.4 REFRIGERANT. R-1233zd(E) or approved low-GWP equivalent; GWP shall not exceed 5.

2.5 PIPING WELDS. Chilled water piping welds at chiller connections: 10 percent radiographic examination per ASME B31.9; acceptance criteria zero porosity in any examined joint. Weld maps and RT reports are quality records.

2.6 VIBRATION. Peak-to-peak shaft vibration shall not exceed 4 mils at any load point, measured per ISO 10816-3 during commissioning.

23 64 26 Part 3 — EXECUTION

3.1 START-UP. Manufacturer-supervised start-up; trend logs at 25/50/75/100 percent load steps submitted to the Commissioning Authority.

3.2 COMMISSIONING. Participation in Level 3 pre-functional and Level 4 functional performance tests per Section 01 91 13.

SECTION 26 33 53 - STATIC UNINTERRUPTIBLE POWER SUPPLY
MERIDIAN-1 DATA CENTRE, HALL A

Project No. MER-1-2026 · Issued for Construction · Revision 0 · 2026-01-15

SECTION 26 33 53 — STATIC UNINTERRUPTIBLE POWER SUPPLY (UPS)

26 33 53 Part 1 — GENERAL

1.1 SUMMARY. This Section specifies double-conversion static UPS systems serving the Hall A critical bus, including battery systems, battery cabinets, and accessories. Equipment tags UPS-A1 through UPS-A4.

1.2 REFERENCES. IEC 62040-3, IEEE 485, IS 1893 (Part 4), NFPA 70E.

1.3 SUBMITTALS. Product data, factory test reports, seismic anchorage calculations, and battery sizing calculations per IEEE 485. The Contractor shall identify any deviation from this Section on the transmittal; undeclared deviations discovered during review shall be grounds for rejection without further evaluation.

26 33 53 Part 2 — PRODUCTS

2.1 EFFICIENCY. UPS efficiency in double-conversion (VFI) mode shall be not less than 97.0 percent at 100 percent rated load, verified per IEC 62040-3. Eco-mode or bypass-mode efficiency figures shall not be substituted for VFI figures.

2.2 BATTERY AUTONOMY. The battery system shall provide not less than 10 minutes autonomy at full rated UPS load, at end-of-life capacity, at 25 degrees C.

2.3 INPUT HARMONICS. Input current total harmonic distortion (THDi) shall not exceed 5 percent at 100 percent load. Compliance shall be achieved by the UPS rectifier alone, without external or optional harmonic filter modules.

2.4 OVERLOAD. The inverter shall sustain 125 percent load for 10 minutes and 150 percent load for 60 seconds.

2.5 PARALLELING. Units shall be capable of paralleling in systems of not less than six units in N+1 configuration without a centralised static switch.

2.6 BATTERY CABINETS. Battery cabinets shall be seismically anchored per IS 1893 (Part 4). Anchor bolt torque records shall be submitted as a quality record prior to energisation.

2.7 EFFICIENCY VERIFICATION. Factory witness test per IEC 62040-3; see also any Addendum modifying the referenced edition.

26 33 53 Part 3 — EXECUTION

3.1 FACTORY TESTS. Full-load heat run, efficiency verification at 25/50/75/100 percent load, transfer tests, and battery discharge test witnessed by the Owner's representative.

3.2 SITE ACCEPTANCE. Level 4 component verification and Level 5 integrated systems test participation per Section 01 91 13.
