SECTION 26 33 53

STATIC UNINTERRUPTIBLE POWER SUPPLY

PART 1 - GENERAL

PART 1 - GENERAL requirements are specified here.

PART 2 - PRODUCTS

26 33 53 Part 2.1.2.A The UPS system shall be configured with N+2 redundancy.

26 33 53 Part 2.3.4 Inverter efficiency shall be not less than 96.0 percent at full load.

26 33 53 Part 2.3.1.E Battery runtime at full load shall be a minimum of 10.0 minutes.

26 33 53 Part 3.4.2.C Battery runtime at full load shall be a minimum of 12 minutes.

26 33 53 Part 2.2.4.C The UPS system shall achieve a minimum COP of 5.2.
