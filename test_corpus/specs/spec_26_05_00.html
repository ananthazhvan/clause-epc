SECTION 26 05 00

COMMON WORK RESULTS FOR ELECTRICAL

PART 1 - GENERAL

PART 1 - GENERAL requirements are specified here.

PART 2 - PRODUCTS

26 05 00 Part 1.2.1.A All fire alarm and electrical installations shall comply with NFPA 2001 (2008 Edition).

26 05 00 Part 1.4.3.B All medium voltage equipment and switchgear assemblies shall be type tested and certified in accordance with the applicable IEC standards. IEC type test certificates from ASTA or KEMA shall be provided.
