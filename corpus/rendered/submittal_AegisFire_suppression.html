Product Datasheet: Aegis CleanAgent ECS-500

The Aegis CleanAgent ECS-500 is an engineered gaseous fire extinguishing suppression system designed for the protection of server rooms. It utilizes the clean agent gas FK-5-1-12 stored in high pressure cylinders.

AEGISFIRE SYSTEMS COMPLIANCE MATRIX Aegis CleanAgent ECS-500

Clause-by-Clause Conformance Statement

Below is our conformance statement indicating compliance with each clause of Section 21 22 00 of the construction specifications.

Spec Clause Parameter / Requirement Conformance Claim Remarks / Evidence
21 22 00 Part 1.2.1 FIRE-STD-EDITION NC Non-Conformant. Submittal states compliance with NFPA 2001 (2008 Edition). The project specification requires NFPA 2001 (2018 Edition), which contains critical safety amendments regarding agent hold times.
21 22 00 Part 1.2.3 FIRE-STANDARDS-ISO NC Non-Conformant. Submittal compliance matrix notes that cylinders are certified to ISO 9809-1 only. The specification mandates certification under UL 2166 and FM 5600 standards.
21 22 00 Part 1.3.3 FIRE-TEST-CERTIFICATE NC Non-Conformant. The vendor submittal package does not contain the required UL/FM factory test and compliance certificate for the clean agent cylinder valves.
21 22 00 Part 2.1.1 FIRE-AGENT-TYPE C Conforms. Suppressant agent is FK-5-1-12, matching the specified FK-5-1-12 clean agent.
21 22 00 Part 2.2.1 FIRE-DESIGN-CONCENTRATION C Conforms. Design concentration is 4.7% (v/v), which matches the specified design basis.
21 22 00 Part 2.2.2 FIRE-NOAEL-LIMIT C Conforms. Design concentration of 4.7% is below the NOAEL safety threshold of 10.0% (v/v), providing an acceptable safety margin.
21 22 00 Part 2.2.3.A FIRE-DISCHARGE-TIME NC Non-Conformant. Main data table claims a 9.2s discharge time, but footnote 2 on page 5 reveals that 'discharge time extends to 11.0 seconds when piping runs exceed 45 meters', violating the NFPA 2001 maximum limit of 10 seconds.
21 22 00 Part 2.2.3.B FIRE-HOLD-TIME NC Non-Conformant. Vendor compliance matrix states the agent hold time (retention) is 9 minutes (540 seconds) under worst-case room leakage, failing the spec minimum requirement of 10 minutes (600 seconds).
21 22 00 Part 2.2.5 FIRE-AGENT-QUANTITY NC Non-Conformant. Total clean agent quantity detailed in the bill of materials is 11,800 kg. Design calculations in the specification mandate a minimum of 12,500 kg of agent to maintain design concentration across all zones.
21 22 00 Part 2.3.1.A FIRE-CYLINDER-PRESSURE NC Non-Conformant. Submittal provides a 360 psi ECS system. Spec requires 500 psi ECS-500 system configuration for optimal high-pressure gas distribution.
21 22 00 Part 2.3.4.F FIRE-NOAEL-MARGIN NC Non-Conformant. The NOAEL safety margin calculation is missing from the vendor submittal package.
