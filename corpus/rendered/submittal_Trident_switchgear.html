Product Datasheet: Trident T-2000

The Trident T-2000 is a modular, type-tested metal-clad air-insulated medium voltage switchgear panel rated for 12kV service, continuous bus current of 2000A, and short circuit withstand rating of 31.5kA for 3 seconds.
