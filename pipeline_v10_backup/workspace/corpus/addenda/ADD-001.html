MERIDIAN-1 DATA CENTRE, HALL A — ADDENDUM NO. 1 (ADD-001)

Date: 2026-03-02 · Issued by the Engineer. This Addendum forms part of the Contract Documents and modifies the Issued-for-Construction specifications as noted. Submittals reviewed against superseded requirements shall be revised and resubmitted.

Item 1 — Section 23 64 26, Clause 2.1 PERFORMANCE

Delete "not less than 6.0" and substitute "not less than 6.3". Revised clause reads: Coefficient of performance (COP) at AHRI 550/590 standard rating conditions shall be not less than 6.3 at 100 percent load.

Item 2 — Section 26 33 53, Clause 2.7 EFFICIENCY VERIFICATION

The referenced test method is amended to IEC 62040-3:2021, with efficiency verified at unity output power factor.
